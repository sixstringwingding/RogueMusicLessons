Medford’s Premier Music Studio

Established in Medford, Oregon in 2008, our teachers have helped thousands of students reach their musical potential. Private, one-on-one classes are available Monday through Friday at our studios in downtown Medford. We also offer in-home lessons in the Medford and Grants Pass areas.

Teachers are available for almost every instrument. Piano, guitar, vocals, violin, ukulele, bass, we do all that and more. Call (541) 292-4247 to schedule a FREE evaluation today!

One-on-One Classes

Our standard membership includes a 30 min. lesson each week with your own private instructor. Your teacher will learn your unique musical goals and tailor our curriculum to fit your learning style.

Professional Instructors

All of our teachers are classically trained local musicians with a solid understanding of music theory. We work with toddlers up through adults in their retirement and beyond!

Affordable Tuition

Our rates are the lowest around and we have huge family and group discounts. We even offer a free lesson to all interested students so you can check out the studio risk-free!
